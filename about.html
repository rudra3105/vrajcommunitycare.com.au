Who We are

Experts in delivering quality disability support services.

At Vraj Community Care, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional, person-centered care to individuals with complex support needs. With a passionate team of professionals and years of industry experience, our mission is to empower lives by offering compassionate, reliable, and tailored disability support services that make a real difference. We understand that every individual is unique, and so are their care requirements. That’s why we work closely with our clients and their families to develop personalized care plans that support independence, build capacity, and promote inclusion in the community. From in-home disability care and pediatric support to assistance with catastrophic injuries and respite care, our services are designed to meet a wide range of needs. We pride ourselves on our responsive support, 24/7 availability, and commitment to ensuring dignity, respect, and safety in every service we deliver. Our team includes trained support workers, care coordinators, therapists, and administrative staff – all aligned with a shared vision: to create a better quality of life for every person we serve. We’re more than a care provider – we’re your partners in living a life full of possibility.

Our Values

We are guided by compassion, integrity, and respect. Every interaction is rooted in empathy, and we strive to create an inclusive environment where each person feels valued, heard, and supported.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in disability support services, transforming lives through innovative care solutions and building stronger, more inclusive communities.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, person-focused disability care that empowers individuals to live with dignity, independence, and purpose — every day, in every way.

What we’re Offering

Providing expert care through specialized disability support services.

Specialist Disability Care

Our team provides round-the-clock, expert care tailored to individuals with complex and high-support needs, ensuring safety, dignity, and enhanced quality of life.

Pediatric In-Home Care

We offer compassionate and professional care for children with disabilities in the comfort of their homes, supporting families with specialized pediatric support.

Catastrophic Injuries Support

Our experienced staff assist individuals recovering from severe, life-altering injuries by delivering consistent physical, emotional, and daily living support.

Respite Care

We provide flexible respite care services, giving families and caregivers the opportunity to rest while their loved ones receive attentive and respectful care.

Free Wheelchair Van Hire

We offer complimentary wheelchair-accessible van hire to support safe, convenient transport for individuals with mobility challenges.

Shared Management

Our shared management service empowers individuals and families to co-manage their supports while we take care of the complexities — giving you control with guidance.
